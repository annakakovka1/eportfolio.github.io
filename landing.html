About Me

As an aspiring MSc Data Science student, my professional journey has been marked by a proactive and results-oriented approach, thriving in fast-paced, dynamic environments. I’m adaptable, resourceful, and committed to continuous improvement—traits that help me navigate complex challenges with ease.

Throughout my career, I’ve honed team-coordination skills, leveraging strong communication and collaboration to build effective relationships. My strategic mindset and attention to detail enable me to identify and mitigate risks while addressing project challenges head-on.

Across educational and commercial settings, I’ve contributed to successful projects, particularly in educational and small-business product development. Notably, I supported initiatives backed by the EU-funded Innovation Bridges program, providing practical support and project coordination.

As I pursue an MSc in Data Science, I’m eager to deepen my expertise in data analysis, machine learning, and predictive modeling—aiming to become a data scientist who delivers meaningful, measurable impact.

My professional ethos revolves around proactive problem-solving and tangible results. I’m driven by curiosity, disciplined execution, and a passion for harnessing data to inform better decisions and create value.
